London iconic fashion brand

Our iconic Falabella is the original vegan luxury handbag. Each is thoughtfully handcrafted by artisans in Italy using next-gen sustainable material innovations.
Named after Stella McCartney's favourite species of horse, the cruelty-free outline is not only inspired by nature, but designed to protect it.
The story of the Falabella is the story of Stella McCartney. Debuting as a rebellion against the notion that luxury requires leather,
it has become a vehicle for vegan innovation and collaboration evolving with and embodying our mission of never compromising on desirability for sustainability.
Today, it is crafted materials ranging from grapes and apples to recyclable plant-based alternatives to animal leather, with monogrammed linings giving a second life to ocean plastics.
